## tidyrhrv

`tidyrhrv` is an R package for reading, iteratively filtering, and analyzing the time component of multiple heart rate variability datasets at once. This work supported reproducible HRV processing and downstream autonomic phenotyping analyses in collaborative Mount Sinai research.

- **Repository**: [Steven-LR/tidyrhrv](https://github.com/steven-lr/tidyrhrv)
- **Associated paper**: Lawrence S, Robinson-Papp J, Kwon P. High-throughput cleaning of raw ECG data. [J Neurol Sci, 2021](https://www.jns-journal.com/article/S0022-510X(21)01556-2/fulltext)
- **Related paper**: Lawrence S, Mueller BR, Kwon P, Robinson-Papp J. [Phenotyping autonomic neuropathy using principal component analysis](https://www.jns-journal.com/article/S0022-510X(21)01558-6/fulltext)
